Tooth Extraction

Tooth Extraction in Kolkata | Safe & Painless Removal

While we always strive to save natural teeth, there are situations where tooth extraction becomes the best option. At Sushmita's Dental Clinic in Salt Lake City (Bidhannagar), Kolkata, we perform both simple and surgical extractions with minimal discomfort.

Reasons for extraction include severe decay, advanced gum disease causing loose teeth, overcrowding before orthodontic treatment, fractured teeth, and impacted wisdom teeth. Our priority is always patient comfort and quick recovery.

Following extraction, we will discuss tooth replacement options including dental implants, bridges, or partial dentures to restore your smile and prevent bone loss.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is tooth extraction painful?

With modern local anaesthesia, the extraction itself is painless. Post-operative discomfort for 24-48 hours is normal.

How long does recovery take?

Simple extractions typically heal within 1-2 weeks.

What can I eat after extraction?

Soft foods like yoghurt, soup, and mashed potatoes for the first 24-48 hours.

Do I need to replace an extracted tooth?

We strongly recommend replacing extracted teeth to prevent bone loss and teeth drifting.
