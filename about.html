Our Story

Dedicated to Dental Excellence in Kolkata

Sushmita's Dental Clinic is led by Dr. Sushmita Roy, BDS, MDS (WBUHS), a highly qualified dental specialist dedicated to providing exceptional oral care to families across Kolkata. Dr. Roy combines advanced clinical expertise with a compassionate, patient-first approach.

Dr. Sushmita Roy's Qualifications & Experience:

  • BDS, MDS — West Bengal University of Health Sciences (WBUHS)
  • Ex House Surgeon, R Ahmed Dental College & Hospital
  • Ex House Surgeon, Calcutta Medical College & Hospital
  • Attached to Anondolok Hospital, Kolkata

Our clinic is equipped with the latest dental technology including digital X-rays, RVG sensors, dental operating microscopes, and modern sterilisation systems. We maintain the highest standards of infection control to ensure the safety of every patient.

We believe that every patient deserves personalised, compassionate care. Dr. Roy and her team take time to listen to your concerns, explain treatment options clearly, and ensure you are comfortable every step of the way.
